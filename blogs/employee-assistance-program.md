The corporate world is evolving and changing at an incredible pace. It is modernizing so that employees are finding it hard to cope with the pressure that has drained them in the long run.

To help employees with all the pressure, organizations can install employee assistance programs. Such programs have helped organizations to reduce turnover rates and [increase productivity](/en/blog/employee-productivity/) as a whole.

## What are Employee Assistance Programs or EAPs?

![What-is-employee-assistance-program](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/01/What-is-employee-assistance-program.png)

Employee Assistance Programs help employees deal with their problems that impact their job. It is a counseling and consulting program where employees put forward the problems they are facing.

And in return, experts provide the right set of solutions. Not only does it help [employees psychology](/en/blog/psychological-safety/), but it also helps them improve their [engagement levels](/en/blog/employee-engagement/) in the long run.

Assistance can be of various forms like-

- Financial help
- Improving work relationships
- Boost in performance
- Mental health issues
- Help with substance abuse, child and elder care

## ***Why Employee Assistance Programs?***

Supporting your employees is always a crucial part of building an influential company culture. And you can enhance your efforts if you can install EAPs in your organization. The main features of an ideal EAPs are below-

***Accessibility-*** EAPs can be accessed online. These programs provide an online platform where employees can log in and access various podcasts, videos, and interactive programs according to their needs.

***Availability-*** These programs are available to employees 24x7 and their immediate family members. The family members need to be eligible for the program. Employees can talk with experts through texts, emails and get help whenever they want.

***Confidential-*** One of the most essential elements of these programs is keeping your employees anonymous. No person will be able to breach any information about others which is always a driving factor of any program.

***Secure-*** Anonymity is what people want, and this program makes sure that everyone stays anonymous. This increases the credibility of the program, and employees are more driven towards using such programs.

## Benefits of Employee Assistance Programs

### 1. Reduced Absenteeism

![Reduced-Absenteeism](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/01/Reduced-Absenteeism.png)

The [well-being of employees](/en/blog/training-employees-well-being/) should always be a prime focus of organizations. But if that is something an organization is missing out on, your employees will feel less valued and try to skip work. No organization wants a situation that will create future problems.

To [reduce absenteeism](/en/blog/employee-absenteeism/), organizations can install Employee Assistance Programs where employees can raise their concerns. This will help automate the process and help the organization pinpoint every employee’s concern. Thus, helping them out in every step whenever needed and maintaining their wellbeing.

### 2. Increased Productivity

![Increased-Productivity](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/01/Increased-Productivity.png)

Distraction from personal or professional problems is the primary productivity killer of your employees. They become less motivated and creative at work which impedes the flow of work.

However, to increase the productivity of employees, an organization can opt for EAPs. These programs help an employee take care of all the difficulties they face. Be it the challenges at work, financial help, or conflicts within the workspace. Thus, reducing unnecessary distractions and helping them focus more on their jobs.

### 3. Positive work environment

![Positive-Work-Environment](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/01/Positive-Work-Environment.png)

A [working environment](/en/blog/ways-to-improve-workplace-environment/) where employees are stressed is not a healthy environment. Productivity drops, and employees tend to leave organizations with such environments, which is never a good sign.

But employees in a less stressful working environment are more loyal and have better concentration. There are many factors to this. One such factor is the implementation of Employee Assistance Programs.

These programs assist a troubling employee by providing support with the issues that they are facing. This results in a change in mood and a reduction in stress levels, thus having positive affect.

### 4. Less turnover

![Less-Turnover](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/01/Less-Turnover.png)

[Turnover](/en/blog/employee-turnover/) has always posed a significant threat to organizations in the corporate world. It costs the company a considerable amount of money, impacting the revenue and market presence in the long run.

Heeding onto the employees' problem can reduce turnover. This is where EAPs come into place to make things easier for organizations. It helps to point out the issues faced by employees, but it also provides the necessary solutions. This makes the job easier for both employers and employees.

Thus, resulting in reduced turnovers as employees feel valued that the organization is taking the essential steps to deal with the problems.

### 5. Improves Morale

![Improves-Morale](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/01/Improves-Morale.png)

You might have employees in your organization who performed well. But there is a significant drop in their morale and quality of work. If they are struggling to lift their confidence and morale, then your employees might be facing some significant hurdles in their life.

EAPs play a significant role in clarifying the problems that your employees might be facing in such circumstances.

Once you get a better understanding of their issues with the help of EAPs, you will assist them in solving them. This not only improves your [employees’ morale](/en/blog/employee-morale-boosters/), but it also increases their working capabilities.

## Implementation of Employee Assistance Programs

Employee Assistance Programs are quite complicated if you do not have the root knowledge of how it works. And implementation is even harder if there a lack of a systematic approach.

We will discuss a few points on how an organization can install an EAP.

### 1. Plan the Structure

![Plan-the-Structure](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/01/Plan-the-Structure.png)

A rigid and firm foundation of any building is their stronghold, and in the very same way, a proper structure for your EAP will prove to be vital.

Before setting out to implement an EAP, plan a working and easy-to-understand design of how the program needs to be. Try to set targets and goals you want to achieve with EAPs in the organization.

Once everything is set with a clear sight of the objectives, you will find it much easier to onboard EAPs without any hassle. And the organization will be able to maximize the program's effectiveness by bringing necessary changes with a planned structure.

Furthermore, the management will have a thorough idea about how the program will work when they stick with the prompt and firm action plan.

### 2. Mind the Budget

![Mind-the-budget](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/01/Mind-the-budget.png)

Employee Assistance Programs are available for any business size. Be it the big corporations or small businesses. But the major difference comes when the budget plays its role in adopting the program.

Big corporations might go for lucrative EAPs, but that option gets diminished for small businesses. This is why adopting EAPs under the budget is very crucial. You might want to provide all the necessary assistance to your employees without burning a big hole in the yearly budget.

### 3. Select the Vendor

![Select-the-Vendor](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/01/Select-the-Vendor.png)

One of the most important factors while implementing an EAP is selecting the right vendor to suit your organization. There are many vendors in the market, but which one will fit best for the organization depends on what you need.

While choosing the vendor, you can look out for certain parameters like-

- Years of service, experience, and genuine testimonials.
- Cost of the vendor within your budget.
- Scope of services covered by the EAP.
- Geographical accessibility.
- Availability of services 24x7.
- Good [customer service and satisfaction levels](/en/blog/employee-satisfaction-to-customer-satisfaction/).

The parameters can differ according to organizational needs. But do shortlist all the necessities so that the EAP vendor covers all your areas of interest.

### 4. Flexibility

![Flexibility](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/01/Flexibility.png)

The flexibility of a program makes them more successful and sustainable in the long run.

What does flexibility signify when implementing an EAP?

Well, the answer is simple. You cannot expect a program to run with the solutions you started from. Modernization and changes will be  made to make the program more effective. Now with a program that uses outdated services will prove out to be a liability.

But, if your EAP allows flexibility, you can make periodic changes and include services with the changing trend. Making an EAP program much effective and helpful to the organization in the long run.

### 5. Introduction and Employees understanding of the Program

![Employees-Understanding-the-Program](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/01/Employees-Understanding-the-Program.png)

The implementation of EAP will not be complete if you miss out on the process of introducing it to your employees. Once employees get the framework of what an EAP consists of, make them understand the benefits it can bring to their lives.

Make sure to conduct an FAQ session where employees can raise questions and doubts about EAP. Have an expert panel ready to answer any questions so that employees can understand EAPs and make full use of them for their benefits.

The more benefits they will receive, the better their satisfaction levels will help the company’s bottom line with [enhanced productivity](/en/blog/work-from-home-tips/) and [engagement](/en/blog/employee-engagement/)

## Summing it up

The program will take time to become successful within the organization. But it is also an asset that helps employees deal with their everyday problems that provide an opportunity to boost their morale and productivity. The workspace should be a place where every employee feels at home and look forward to being their best.
