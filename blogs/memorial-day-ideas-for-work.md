Memorial Day is usually considered the unofficial start of summer when everyone starts to plan for long trips or family outings. However, it is much more important than celebrating the start of the summer. It’s a time to honor the military personnel who laid down their lives while serving the military. And if you are planning to celebrate it in the workplace, then this article will assist you in providing some fantastic memorial day ideas for work.

Let’s delve in.

## What is Memorial Day?

Memorial Day honors the men and women who died while serving their country in the military, particularly those who died in battle or due to battle wounds. In other words, the purpose of Memorial Day is to honor the veterans who made the ultimate sacrifice for the sake of their country.

## When is Memorial Day 2022?

Memorial Day is celebrated every year on the last Monday of May. In 2022, it will be celebrated on the 30th of May.

## Why Do We Need To Celebrate Memorial Day At Work?

The celebration of Memorial Day at work is important so that the present generation has a better understanding of the sacrifices made by the war veterans. This particular day is seen as a national holiday. However, you can still commemorate Memorial Day at work and demonstrate its significance to your employees. The main goal is to educate them about this auspicious day and to assist them in paying proper tribute to war veterans.

Memorial Day is considered the National Moment of Remembrance, where everyone in the country pays tribute to the men and women who were part of the liberation struggle.

Here is a list of a few ideas that you can invest in and make the day even more memorable at work.

## 9 Memorial Day Ideas To Celebrate At the Workplace

### 1. Create a Display

![Create-a-Display](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/05/Create-a-Display.png)

To commemorate the people who laid down their lives while working for the military, you can create a wall or virtual website display with all the names of the martyrs. This will demonstrate your support for the armed forces and help your employees understand the sacrifices they go through to protect the nation.

Furthermore, you can distribute Memorial Day booklets that highlight its inception and significance in the country’s history. It is one of the simplest yet most effective Memorial Day ideas for work.

### 2. Gift Poppies to Everyone

![Gift-Poppies-to-Everyone](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/05/Gift-Poppies-to-Everyone.png)

Poppy flowers have a strong association with Memorial Day. It is especially used on this day to honor the fallen soldiers for their ultimate sacrifice. To set the tone for this day, give your employees paper poppies to wear like a badge on their shirts. This will make employees feel more involved in Memorial Day activities and instill a sense of honor in them.

### 3. Take Your Employee to a Parade

![Take-Your-Employee-to-a-Parade](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/05/Take-Your-Employee-to-a-Parade.png)

Another great Memorial Day idea for your workplace is to take the employees to the annual parade. The parade aims to draw Americans' attention to the true meaning of the holiday: honoring those who have served and died to protect the country's liberty. You can arrange for their transportation and lodging so that the employees can fully enjoy the parade.

### 4. Memorial Day themed Dress

![Memorial-Day-themed-Dress](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/05/Memorial-Day-themed-Dress.png)

Memorial Day is a special occasion where you can show your patriotism towards the country. One of the best ways to celebrate it is by setting a dress code that resonates with the national flag, i.e., it includes primary colors such as red, white, and blue. Request that your employees dress creatively for Memorial Day and come to work wearing outfits that perfectly reflect the spirit of the holiday.

### 5. Veteran Lunch

![Veteran-Lunch](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/05/Veteran-Lunch.png)

A good Memorial Day workplace idea is to plan or [schedule a lunch](/en/blog/best-team-lunch-ideas/) for war veterans. Ask your employees to compile a list of coworkers whose friends or family members may have served in the military.

Then gather a team to assist you in planning and hosting a beautiful lunch honoring the special guests, i.e., the veterans. Spending time with war veterans will help your employees to respect the day and show their appreciation towards the veterans.

### 6. Office Decoration

![Office-Decoration](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/05/Office-Decoration.png)

If your workplace appears dull on such an important day, the spirit of Memorial Day will be lost. To commemorate this occasion, you can decorate your office in patriotic colors and play the music that fits in well with Memorial Day.

Furthermore, you can invite special guests, such as war veterans, and tell them how valuable they are to the country and the people.

### 7. Social Media Drive

![Social-Media-Drive](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/05/Social-Media-Drive.png)

Another excellent way for a workplace to promote Memorial Day is through social media. Share your office decorations or a luncheon with veterans on social media and encourage your employees to do the same. Ask them to become the active [employee advocates](/en/blog/employee-advocacy/) on behalf of the company.

You can also share stories or photos of family members that served in the military and acknowledge them for their sacrifice. You can also share an appreciation post for the employees who were an integral part of the Memorial Day celebrations in the organization.

### 8. Organize Volunteering Activities

![Organize-Volunteering-Activities](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/05/Organize-Volunteering-Activities.png)

To keep the spirit of Memorial Day alive, encourage employees to volunteer for NGOs which provide aid to veterans. You can also contribute financially to such NGOs and participate in goodwill initiatives collaborating with your company.

Furthermore, do not treat such initiatives as a one-time event. If you are prepared, make it a part of your organizational norms and plan frequent volunteer activities to thank veterans for their exceptional service.

### 9. Offer Special Thanks to a Veteran

![Offer-Special-Thanks-to-a-Veteran](https://blogimage.vantagecircle.com/content/images/2022/05/Offer-Special-Thanks-to-a-Veteran.png)

One of the Memorial Day ideas for work that you can implement is to express your heartfelt gratitude to veterans by organizing a public event in their honor. You can thank them for their years of service by gifting some well-thought-out care packages.

Involve your employees and give them the task of assisting the veterans during the event. It will help your employees develop some undervalued [interpersonal skills](/en/blog/interpersonal-skills/) such as empathy, awareness, and patience.

## Summing it Up!

The list of Memorial Day ideas for work provided above will help you get a head start on commemorating the day. Furthermore, you can expand on these ideas to make the day even more memorable and eventful so that everyone remembers it fondly for the rest of their lives.

From memorial day to [easter](/en/blog/easter-gifts-for-employees/), there are a lot of chances for you to make your [work culture](/en/blog/workplace-culture/) fun and engaging for your employees. You just need to be proactive while focusing on [increasing productivity](/en/blog/employee-productivity/) of the workforce.
